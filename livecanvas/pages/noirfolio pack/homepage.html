THE PROCESS

Create Your Project 5 Simple steps

We follow a structured approach to build a website that is customized to meet the specific needs of your business.

2 hours

Are We a Good Fit?

Exploration Call

During our initial conversation, we will explore whether I can assist you with your needs. We'll discuss your expectations for a new website, the reasons behind this necessity, your objectives, and the challenges we can address through the new site.

  • We will familiarize ourselves with each other
  • Identify the best ways I can support you
  • Clarify your website goals

12 hours

We need to establish a clear plan for moving forward.

Concept & Strategy

Before moving forward, it's essential to outline the concept and strategy that will guide the creation of your new website. What key messages do you want to convey? How do you envision your brand being represented online? What strategies will we implement to engage your target audience effectively?

  • We will explore your brand's core values and mission.
  • We will define your target audience and their needs.
  • We will develop a strategic plan to enhance user experience and drive conversions.

21 hours

Project Overview

Website Design Consultation

Welcome to our Website Design Consultation service. Our goal is to provide you with insights and direction to create a website that resonates with your target audience. We will discuss your vision, preferences, and how we can elevate your online presence to meet your business goals.

  • Understand your design preferences and requirements
  • Discuss your target audience and key messaging
  • Outline a roadmap for your website development process
35 hours

New Opportunities Await

Project Initialization

During the development phase of your web project, our team focuses on turning the design concepts into a functional website. This is where we write the code, implement features, and integrate various components to bring your vision to life. What functionalities are essential for your website? How can we streamline the user experience to ensure that your audience engages effectively with your content? What challenges do you foresee during the development process, and how can we address them together?

  • We will collaborate closely to ensure all features align with your goals
  • Regular updates and communication will keep you informed
  • Testing and feedback will refine the website before launch
